Dad made Creamy Cucumber Soup


Sounds GROSS but might be delicious.

Here is the recipe to find out!


INGREDIENTS

  • 2 large cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 sweet onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sea salt

DRESSING

  • 1 cup mayonnaise, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

DIRECTIONS

  1. Mix cucumbers, onion and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, dill, garlic podwer, and pepper
    for dressing together in a bowl until smooth. Pour over the cucumber mixture and
    stir until coated.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours before serving

ENJOY!!
